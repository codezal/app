Send a desktop (OS) notification to the user.

Use this to pull the user's attention back when something is worth knowing now and they may have stepped away — for example: a long-running task finished, a build or test run completed, or you have hit a decision that blocks further progress and need their input.

Parameters:
- `title`: short headline (lead with what the user would act on).
- `body` (optional): one extra line of detail.

Guidance:
- Be sparing. A notification the user did not need is annoying. Do not notify for routine progress or for things they are clearly watching happen in real time.
- Keep it short and write it in the user's language.
- This does not replace your normal chat reply — it is an out-of-band ping, not the answer itself.
